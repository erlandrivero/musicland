## 📋 Detailed Content Summary

### Application Overview

**AI Music Studio** is a professional web-based platform that uses artificial intelligence to generate studio-quality music tracks in under 60 seconds. The application serves content creators, podcasters, marketers, and musicians who need custom, commercially-licensed music without musical expertise.

### Technical Implementation

- **Framework**: Next.js 14 with TypeScript

- **Authentication**: NextAuth.js v5 with Google OAuth and Magic Link

- **AI Integration**: SunoAPI for music generation

- **Database**: MongoDB (production) / SQLite (development)

- **Deployment**: Netlify with live URL

- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS with a custom design system

### Key APIs Documented

1. **SunoAPI** - Core AI music generation functionality

1. **NextAuth.js v5** - Authentication and session management

1. **Google OAuth API** - Social authentication

1. **MongoDB/SQLite API** - Data persistence

1. **Stripe API** - Payment processing (planned integration)
